🚛 Welcome

Welcome to Shoreline Freight.

Your job is to deliver packages safely, efficiently, and accurately.

Professional standard starts on Day 1

🎯 Your Daily Goal

  • 100% delivery accuracy
  • 20–30 stops per hour
  • Zero safety violations

Speed matters, but safety comes first.

🔧 Pre-Trip Check

  • Inspect tires, lights, brakes, and mirrors
  • Check fuel level
  • Confirm scanner and phone are charged
  • Do not leave until inspection is complete

📦 Loading the Truck

  • Load packages in stop order
  • Group by street, zone, or apartment complex
  • Keep your next stops easy to reach

Messy truck = slow day.

🚪 Every Delivery

  1. Park safely
  2. Grab the correct package
  3. Check the address twice
  4. Deliver to a safe location
  5. Scan the package
  6. Take a photo if required

⚠️ Non-Negotiable Rules

  • Seatbelt at all times
  • No phone use while driving
  • No unsafe rushing
  • Never enter customer homes
  • Always scan the package

🐕 Real-World Situations

  • Dog outside: stay calm, do not run, return to truck if needed
  • No safe drop spot: mark undelivered
  • Upset customer: stay professional, do not argue

🧠 Efficiency Mode

  • Stay on one side of the street
  • Group nearby stops
  • Walk fast, do not run
  • Avoid doubling back

Smart drivers save time without cutting corners.

🌙 End of Day

  • All packages delivered or accounted for
  • Undelivered packages coded correctly
  • Truck cleared out
  • Equipment returned

🧪 Quick Quiz

1. What is the first thing before driving?

2. What do you do if a dog is outside?

3. Which matters more: speed or safety?

4. When do you scan a package?

5. Can you enter a customer's home?

Answer key: pre-trip inspection; stay calm/do not run; safety; every package at every delivery; no.
